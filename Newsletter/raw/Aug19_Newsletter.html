August 2019 Newsletter

This is a monthly newsletter of PSL model releases, events, and updates.
Subscribe at https://www.pslmodels.org and follow us on Twitter @PSLModels
User Highlights

ParamTools: Now provides out-of-the-box parameter indexing for projects that have parameters that change at some rate (e.g. inflation) over time. Link

PSL in India: Rick Evans and Jason DeBacker held a week of training at the Tax Policy Research Unit in New Delhi, India focusing on open source models built off of OG-USA and Tax-Calculator. Link

PSL meeting: Cody Kallen presented Business-Taxation, a PSL-incubating model for business tax policy evaluation, at the August PSL meeting. Link

OG-USA: Documentation now hosted online. Link

Tax-Calculator, Tax-Brain, OG-USA, and the Cost-of-Capital-Calculator: Feature a new income tax parameter, called a refundable payroll tax credit (RPTC), that can be used to emulate a payroll tax exemption.

Cost-of-Capital-Calculator

Evaluates the effect of US federal taxes on businesses' investment incentives

Releases and new features
  • Release 1.0.4
    • Updates web application interface.

OG-USA

OG-USA is an overlapping-generations model for evaluating fiscal policy in the United States

Releases and new features
  • Allows users to define the distribution of government transfers and allows those distribution amounts to vary over time (PR #479).
  • Adds more functions to produce output tables and plots (PR #483).
  • Moves documentation online. Link

ParamTools

ParamTools is a library for parameter processing and validation with a focus on computational modeling projects

Releases and new features
  • Release 0.8.0
    • Adds out-of-the-box parameter indexing for projects that have parameters that change at some rate over time (PR #65).

Tax-Calculator

Tax-Calculator is a USA federal individual income and payroll tax microsimulation model

Releases and new features
  • Release 2.5.0
    • Add to the income tax a refundable payroll tax credit (RPTC) that can be used to emulate a payroll tax exemption (PR #2366).
    • Allows reforms that specify a CPI_offset change in the same year as a tax policy parameter's indexing status is changed (PR #2364).
PSL Catalog
  • Behavioral-Responses is a partial-equilibrium behavioral module that works with Tax-Calculator.
  • Cost-of-Capital-Calculator evaluates the effect of US federal taxes on businesses' investment incentives.
  • OG-USA is an overlapping-generations model for evaluating fiscal policy in the United States.
  • ParamTools is a library for parameter processing and validation with a focus on computational modeling projects.
  • Tax-Brain is an integrator package for multiple open source tax models.
  • Tax-Calculator is a USA federal individual income and payroll tax microsimulation model.
